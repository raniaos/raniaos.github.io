Lingkar

iOS Application

  • Technologies used: Local Storage, CloudKit, Geo Location, WidgetKit, Notification, 3rd Party Framework Integration (BMKG)
  • Published On: Public TestFlight
  • Project duration: Sep - Dec 2023 (3 months)
  • Project Type: Group (6 people)
  • Role: Developer, UI Designer, UX Designer / Researcher

Brief Description

Lingkar is an all-in-one app designed to enhance family safety during emergencies. Lingkar is a companion app for you to stay connected and informed, ensuring your loved ones are safe and sound, no matter what life throws your way. Features:

  • Provides informations about natural disasters that happen near your circle members and gives emergency alerts for everyone. Lingkar also shows alert when your battery level is low.
  • Make personalized circles for your family members, friends, or any group that matters most to you.
  • Know the location of your circle members, ensuring that your circle members are safe. Lingkar provides real-time weather updates.

Struggles

  • As a team, our struggles were mainly about the Apple technologies, it was our first time using Geo Location, WidgetKit, and Notifications. So as a team, we had to find the usage and the capabilities of the technologies. We had some problems with CloudKit at first because of the delays from CloudKit itself, but we managed to overcome the problems by using Local Storage to help store the data locally.
  • Making an app that is easy to use for both young and elderly people. The app designs kept on changing every time there were feedbacks given from users, mentors, or other teams' members.
  • Other struggle I faced (individually) was using CloudKit. It was my first time using CloudKit, and it was completely different from other databases I know, like MySQL.
